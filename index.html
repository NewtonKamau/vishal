About me

Susan Ocholla is a RYS 200HRS certified Yoga teacher, Level 2 Power Yoga teacher and certified Yin, Kundalini, Ashtanga, and TIMBo (trauma sensitive yoga) instructor. I believe yoga and meditation are continuous and lifelong practices and that we must maintain mental, emotional, and physical flexibility as a sense of personal identity. I challenge my students to grow by meeting both their physical and spiritual edge with friendliness and teaches them to practice listening to their bodies and each breath. I am dedicated to the growth and development of my students both on and off their mats and to relaxation and happiness in their daily life.

Other than teaching yoga, I run a feeding programme for street families and I started it out of compassion and love for the kids
